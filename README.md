## About

Monsters & Girls adds tameable monster-girl companions to Minecraft — mushroom gals, wisps, mandrakes, gourdragoras, and more. Each species has its own behaviour, aesthetics, and bond mechanics.

**Original mod:** [Monsters & Girls](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/monsters-girls) by **b_art_ek** (Forge).  
**This repository:** Fabric port and 1.21.1 rebuild on HZLib by **MSymbios / Heria Zone**.

This is a multi-platform collaboration — b_art_ek owns and develops the Forge version; MSymbios develops the Fabric version with creative freedom granted by the project owner.

## Species

### 🌿 Mandrake (3 families)
Chorus Girl · Flower Girl (sings!) · Fructus Girl

### 🍄 Mushroom Gals (11 families)
Brown · Amanita · Crimson · Infernal · Inkcaps · Molten · Puffball · Fluffball (flies) · Snowball · Soul Wanderer · Warped

Each mushroom family ties to a biome or dimension — found through the Genesis Powder mechanic, tamed with the appropriate food, and will plant their native mushroom nearby once tamed. Some have combat passive abilities via EmanationFeature (Molten ignites targets, Snowball freezes, Soul Wanderer inflicts wither).

### 🎃 Gourdragora (3 families · 3 sizes each)
Golden · Lumina · Jack'o *(October only)*

Pumpkin companions that spawn in mini, default, and big sizes. Carve them with shears. Jack'o only shows up in October.

### 🐝 Bee Gals (5 variants)
Default · Firefly · Pixie · Silk Moth · Black Moth

### 💚 Globberie (3 variants)
Pink · Pink Twintails · Yellow

### 👻 Spook (3 variants)
Cream · Peach · Teal

### 💧 Wisp (3 variants)
Blue · Green · Yellow *(instantly defeats Vex and Phantoms)*

### 🟢 Slime
Blue

### 🧚 Maiden of the Skies
Single variant

## Installation

1. Download Monsters & Girls from CurseForge or Modrinth
2. Download **HZLib** and **GeckoLib**
3. Place all `.jar` files in your `mods` folder
4. Launch Minecraft
